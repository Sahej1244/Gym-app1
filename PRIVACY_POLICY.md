## What we collect

- **Profile information you provide**: age, height, weight, gender, activity
  level, fitness experience, and goal.
- **Usage data you create**: logged meals, weight check-ins, workout history,
  chat messages with the AI coach, and photos you choose to upload for food
  scanning or the body-transformation preview.
- **Technical data**: your IP address (used only for basic rate-limiting to
  prevent abuse — see below), and standard web server logs.

We do **not** collect payment information directly (see Payments below), and
we do not sell your data to third parties.

## How we use it

- To calculate your personalized calorie/macro targets and workout plan.
- To power the AI coach, food photo scanner, and food-estimate features —
  this means the text and images you submit for those features are sent to
  our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a response. See "Third parties" below.
- To prevent abuse (basic rate limiting by IP address).
- To send you a one-time email if you join the Premium waitlist, and for no
  other purpose unless you separately opt in.
